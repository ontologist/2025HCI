Presentation Content (7-10 minutes)

Required Components:
  1. Problem & User Research (1-2 min):
    • What problem did you address?
    • Who are your users?
    • Key research insights
  2. Design Process Journey (2-3 min):
    • Personas and user needs
    • Initial sketches to final design
    • Key design decisions and why
    • Iterations based on feedback
  3. Final Prototype Demo (2-3 min):
    • Live demonstration of working prototype
    • Walk through 1-2 key user flows
    • Highlight unique features
  4. Usability Testing Results (1 min):
    • Key findings from testing
    • Improvements made based on feedback
  5. Reflections & Learnings (1-2 min):
    • What did you learn about HCI?
    • What surprised you?
    • What would you do differently?
    • Connection to HCI principles
