Part 1: Bot Introduction (15 minutes)

  • Access the HCI-101 Bot portal using provided credentials
  • Complete the bot onboarding process
  • Ask the bot about HCI fundamentals
  • Take screenshots of key interactions

Part 2: Interface Analysis (30 minutes)

  • Select 2-3 smart device interfaces to analyze (smartphone apps, smartwatch interfaces, voice assistants, etc.)
  • Examine each interface carefully, noting:
    • What tasks can users accomplish?
    • How easy is it to understand what to do?
    • What design elements make it usable or difficult?
    • How does it compare to desktop interfaces?
  • Take screenshots of interesting design elements
  • Discuss your observations with the bot

Part 3: Reflection Writing (15 minutes)

  • Document your observations in a structured reflection
  • Connect your findings to HCI concepts from the lecture
  • Begin considering potential final project topics
