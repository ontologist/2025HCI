1. Chatbot Analysis Report (2-3 different bots)

  • Analyze 2-3 different chatbots (e.g., customer service bots, virtual assistants)
  • Identify type, use case, personality, and tone
  • Document strengths and weaknesses
  • Include screenshots of conversations

2. Conversation Flow Diagram

  • Complete conversation flow including:
    • Greeting
    • Intent recognition
    • Information gathering
    • Response/Action
    • Closure
  • Both happy path and error scenarios

3. Personality Guidelines

  • Clear personality definition (friendly, professional, playful, etc.)
  • Tone guidelines
  • Example phrases that match the personality
  • What the bot should and shouldn't say

4. Bot Interaction Screenshots

Evidence of HCI-101 Bot guidance throughout the design process
