## Course Description | ã‚³ãƒ¼ã‚¹èª¬æ˜

**Purpose and Learning Philosophy | ç›®çš„ã¨å­¦ç¿’å“²å­¦:**

This course introduces students to Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) principles and practices in the context of modern smart devices and AI-powered interfaces. Through hands-on activities and design thinking methodologies, students learn to create user-centered interfaces for smartphones, tablets, wearables, IoT devices, and conversational AI systems. Using no-code prototyping tools, students experience the complete design process from user research to usability testing, preparing them to design the next generation of human-centered interactive systems.
