## Accessibility Considerations:
## アクセシビリティの考慮事項：

1. **Visual:**
   - High contrast colors for text
   - Large, readable fonts (minimum 24pt)
   - Captions for videos
   - Alt text for images

2. **Auditory:**
   - Transcripts for video content
   - Visual representations of audio concepts
   - Clear speaker audio for voice demos

3. **Language:**
   - Bilingual throughout (English/Japanese)
   - Clear, simple language
   - Technical terms explained
   - Glossary of new terminology

4. **Cognitive:**
   - Clear structure and navigation
   - Consistent formatting
   - Progressive disclosure of information
   - Visual organizers and diagrams
